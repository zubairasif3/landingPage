Science Fiction Films

Science fiction (or sci-fi) is a film genre that uses speculative, fictional science-based depictions of phenomena that are not fully accepted by mainstream science, such as extraterrestrial lifeforms, spacecraft, robots, cyborgs, mutants, interstellar travel, time travel, or other technologies.

Filmmakers use science fiction to reflect our aspirations, terrors, and issues of the times. Through genre lens, we can consider our impact on the environment, technology gone berserk (The Terminator), identity (The Matrix), and societal breakdowns (Invasion of the Body Snatchers). We might even check-in on the current state of the human condition (2001: A Space Odyssey).
